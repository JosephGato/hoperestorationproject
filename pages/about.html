Rural Kenya

Outside of Nairobi, we serve in several remote, rural villages. Most families tend
livestock to earn a living, and the arid climate produces long periods of drought and
food shortages. On average, 85% of people living in these areas are extremely
impoverished, and a very small percentage are literate. Access to quality education and
gainful employment is extremely limited. Early marriage and traditional gender roles
(girls working at home and boys working in the family trade) prevent most kids from
attending school.

Educational Work For Schools

On the one hand, in our training modules, our students shall gain insights into the world
of the „global South “, meaning developing and emerging countries. On the other hand,
they shall understand the global interdependencies of Southern countries from industrialized
countries and simultaneously learn how to reflect their own responsibilities in terms of
consumption and sustainability.

Our Vision

Empowered people transform the world. Together, we work with

the world’s most difficult regions.

Our Approach

committed to respond to humanitarian crises by providing solutions that integrate relief,
recovery and development.Following our vision and mission, we aim to
initiate partnerships with local organizations on the ground and involve the communities
at all stages of the project cycle. Through their participation, we are able to create
ownership and durable solutions for the urgent needs. While doing so, we are
to the humanitarian principles and international standards.

About Us

Every single day, support for the most vulnerable in our communities is needed. We, Vision
Hope International, invest in a future with a life of dignity for everyone – without fear of
violence, poverty or injustice. This future starts with actions of love and hope.
We are an international organization, based in Germany, and provide both emergency relief and long-term,
holistic development assistance in conflict-ridden areas. Our efforts are focused on conflict areas in the
Middle East. With our passion and dedication, we are working towards a sustainable future for individuals, families and
communities.

Our Mission

We work with local communities to create sustainable solutions that advance
peace in the world’s most difficult regions.
